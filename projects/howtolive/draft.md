# How To Live Overview

How To Live is a project focusing on creating informative and practical content on
how to live life, covering topics such as health, productivity, relationships.

## Categories

Content is split up into a couple of particular categories/niches, namely:

* Health
    * Nutrition
    * Exercise
* Productivity
    * Discipline
* Relationships
* Work
* Hobbies

## Format

For starters, content will be created in text form as blog articles, with possibility to
move to videos and/or podcasts in the future.

Focus is on the author to write from their own experience and from a point of view
of the advice being applicable to themselves, like a letter to one's self, generalizing points where possible.
